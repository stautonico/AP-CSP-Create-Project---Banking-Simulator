Security

Ocean Financial Bank is the safest digital bank on the internet. We use 192-bit encryption as well as 128-bit random key to ensure that your account is kept safe. We offer insurance on up to $140,000 if your account ever get compromised. All our employees have endured a rigorous training program to ensure that they understand important security concepts to ensure that every account is as secure as possible. All of our database managers and engineers are required to have the CISM (Certified Information Security Manager) and CompTIA Security+ certifications. This guarantees that our databases are impenetrable.


How Our System Works

Ocean Financial Bank was built with efficiency and security in mind, which means all of the transactions we process are quick and secure. We use fiber optic technology to ensure that your transaction happens as quickly as possible. Every transaction is also secured with end-to-end encryption, meaning your money gets to where it needs to be, 100% of the time. Our customer's privacy and happiness come before anything else, even if we have to work overtime to make it happen.
